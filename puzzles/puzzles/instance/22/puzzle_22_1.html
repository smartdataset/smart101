Problem 22 Instance 1



Question

A boy built a castle using clay and decorated it with a flag. He inserted the flagpole into the highest point of the castle. A half of the flagpole is inside the castle. The upper tip of the flagpole was 79 cm above the ground and the lower tip was 11 cm above the ground. How tall was the castle?

Options

A: 47 cm B: 45 cm C: 49 cm D: 41 cm E: 44 cm
