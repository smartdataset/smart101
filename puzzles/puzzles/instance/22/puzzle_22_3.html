Question

A boy built a castle using clay and decorated it with a flag. He stuck the flagpole into the highest point of the castle. A half of the flagpole is inside the castle. The lower tip of the flagpole was 11 cm above the ground and the upper tip was 91 cm above the ground. How tall was the castle?

Options

A: 52 cm B: 49 cm C: 51 cm D: 54 cm E: 48 cm
