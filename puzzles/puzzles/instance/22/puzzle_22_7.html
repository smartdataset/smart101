Question

A boy decorated a castle he built from clay with a flag. He stuck the flagpole into the highest point of the castle. A half of the flagpole is inside the castle. The upper tip of the flagpole was 75 cm above the ground and the lower tip was 15 cm above the ground. How tall was the castle?

Options

A: 49 cm B: 42 cm C: 46 cm D: 47 cm E: 45 cm
