Problem 22 Instance 8



Question

A boy decorated a castle he built from clay with a flag. He stuck the flagpole into the highest point of the castle. Three quarters of the flagpole are inside the castle. The upper tip of the flagpole was 94 cm above the ground and the lower tip was 18 cm above the ground. How tall was the castle?

Options

A: 72 cm B: 73 cm C: 74 cm D: 75 cm E: 71 cm
