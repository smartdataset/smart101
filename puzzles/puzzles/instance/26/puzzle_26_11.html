Problem 26 Instance 10



Question

We have locks, medals, footwears, writing utensils, envelopes, and disks repeat regularly on a game board. Some juice was spilled on the board. As a result, some of the pictures cannot be seen. These are the gray spaces in the picture. How many medals were on the board before the juice was spilled?

Options

A: 10 B: 14 C: 13 D: 7 E: 12
