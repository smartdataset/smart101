Question

We have clocks, balls, writing utensils, disks, flowers, and medals repeat regularly on a game board. Some juice was spilled on the board. As a result, some of the figures cannot be seen. These are the gray spaces in the figure. How many balls were on the board before the juice was spilled?

Options

A: 5 B: 6 C: 9 D: 11 E: 7
