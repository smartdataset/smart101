Question

A bird jumps from the post on one end to the other end on a fence. He needs 3 seconds for each jump. He makes 9 jumps ahead and then 4 jumps back. Then he again makes 9 jumps ahead and 4 jumps back, and so on. In how many seconds can the bird get from one end to the other end?

Options

A: 72 B: 66 C: 75 D: 51 E: 63
