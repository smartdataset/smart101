Question

A bird jumps from the post on one end to the other end on a fence. Each jump takes him 3 seconds. He makes 9 jumps ahead and then 2 jumps back. Then he again makes 9 jumps ahead and 2 jumps back, and so on. In how many seconds can the bird get from one end to the other end?

Options

A: 78 B: 75 C: 63 D: 72 E: 81
