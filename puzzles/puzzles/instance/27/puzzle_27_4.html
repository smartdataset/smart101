Problem 27 Instance 4



Question

A bird jumps on a fence from the post on one end to the other end. He needs 1 second for each jump. He makes 8 jumps ahead and then 2 jumps back. Then he again makes 8 jumps ahead and 2 jumps back, and so on. There are 25 posts in total. In how many seconds can the bird get from one end to the other end?

Options

A: 36 B: 35 C: 33 D: 38 E: 37
