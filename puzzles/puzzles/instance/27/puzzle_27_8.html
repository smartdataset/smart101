Question

A bird jumps from the post on one end to the other end on a fence, and there are 21 posts in total. He needs 1 second for each jump. He makes 6 jumps ahead and then 1 jump back. Then he again makes 6 jumps ahead and 1 jump back, and so on. In how many seconds can the bird get from one end to the other end?

Options

A: 23 B: 27 C: 28 D: 29 E: 26
