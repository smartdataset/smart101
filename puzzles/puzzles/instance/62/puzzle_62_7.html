Question

He puts sponge as shown. The soy sauce packet ahead of chocolate and mouse pad behind sticky note. Mouse pad behind cup. Leg warmers ahead of sponge and chocolate behind sticky note. On which location can the cup not be placed?

Options

A: 4 B: 7 C: 1 D: 3 E: 5
