Question

Nicholas has 8 toy objects: an eye liner, a checkbook, a thermostat, a purse, a plastic fork, an outlet, a water bottle, and a fork. He puts each toy at a different location of the shelf. He puts checkbook as shown. The purse left of fork. Outlet right of plastic fork and fork right of checkbook. Checkbook left of outlet and purse left of outlet. On which location can the plastic fork not be placed?

Options

A: 8 B: 5 C: 1 D: 2 E: 3
