Question

Anthony wants to write digits [ 2 3 5 6 7 10] inside the 6 squares of the figure. He wants a distinct digit in every square. He wants both the sum of the digits in the white squares and the sum of the digits in the gray squares to be 13. What digit must he write in the square with the question mark?

Options

A: 2 B: 13 C: 6 D: 5 E: 7
