Problem 90 Instance 5



Question

A person has two boxes. There are 10 biscuits in the box on the left and the other box is vacant. From one day later, Robert will put 2 biscuits in the box on the left and 3 in the box on the right every day. In how many days will the two boxes have the same count of biscuits?

Options

A: 2 B: 12 C: 18 D: 5 E: 10
